# Global AI Job Market & Salary Trends 2025

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of the **global AI and Machine Learning job market in 2025**. It focuses on salary patterns, demand across roles, experience levels, company sizes, and regional trends. The analysis is visualized through interactive **Power BI dashboards**, enabling clear and actionable insights.

The project is designed for:

* Aspiring data analysts and AI professionals
* Students exploring AI career opportunities
* Recruiters and business stakeholders analyzing market trends

## Objectives

* Analyze salary distribution across AI and ML job roles
* Understand the impact of experience level on compensation
* Compare salaries by country and region
* Evaluate hiring trends across company sizes
* Identify high-demand and high-paying AI roles

## Dataset Overview

The dataset includes:

* Job Title
* Job ID
* Salary (USD)
* Experience Level
* Company Size
* Employment Type
* Employee Residence
* Country / Location
* Remote Work Ratio
* Required skills
* Education Required
* Years of Experience
* Industry
* Posting date
* Benifits Score
* Company Name

The data represents AI and ML roles across multiple global markets for the year 2025.

## Tools & Technologies Used

* **Power BI** – Data modeling, DAX, and dashboard creation
* **Excel / CSV** – Data cleaning and preprocessing
* **SQL (optional)** – Exploratory data analysis
* **GitHub** – Version control and project documentation

## Dashboard Highlights

* **Salary Distribution Analysis** – Average and median salaries by role
* **Experience vs Salary** – Relationship between experience level and pay
* **Geographic Insights** – Country-wise salary comparison
* **Company Size Analysis** – Compensation trends across startups, mid-size, and large firms
* **Job Market Trends** – Demand patterns for AI-related roles

## Key Insights

* Senior-level AI roles command significantly higher salaries globally
* Large enterprises generally offer higher compensation than startups
* Certain countries consistently lead in AI salary benchmarks
* Experience level is a stronger salary driver than company size in many roles
